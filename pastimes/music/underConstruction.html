Music has been a long time companion to me ! Ever since my childhood days I have been drawn to melodies and could hardly resist them ! I use to try to sing some of the melodies from movies to a decent closeness with the original and most of all I had this great urge to sing and hum to myself all the time . It was not until I realised that there is a way to understand the nuances of music ,from a technical point of view that I was struggling with my own ways of mimicing the melodies not knowing for sure if they were perfect ! I was trained in Karnatic Music and slowly I could see the change in my approach to singing . Rather than a lay man's approach it was very technical and less prone to error !It did polish my crude ways and gave me a more disciplined approach to the subject !
