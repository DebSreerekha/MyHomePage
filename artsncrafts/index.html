As a child I was more busy collecting things of beauty .. like stones , shells , feathers , leaves , flowers , insects.. They were sacrosanct items of creation ..I used to spend time in the admiration of how the things were . I must admit that I have been a late bloomer in the aspect of putting things to use to create far more beauty from existing beauty .. I was more a conservationist , rather than a creativist .. I had the good fortune of meeting many creative people in my life right from mychildhood .. but it didnt seem to captivate my interests then .. but the interactions did leave their footprint in my memory and I seem to find myself chewing the cud now :)and enjoying it thoroughly .
