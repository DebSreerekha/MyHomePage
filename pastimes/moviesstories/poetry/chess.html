Chess

Chess has been one of my most favourite games so far ! As a child I did not play much of it ! But in my twenties it had been the most engaging sport that I indulged in whenever and whereever time permitted ! Even at work during breaks , with my team mates or any chess enthusiast for that matter .. If not playing , being an attentive spectator was also something I could not bring myself to refrain from ! It was like a mad addiction in my twenties ! I happened to win against one of the online chess programs just once in March 2005 and that was my most priced moment , which I shared with my chess enthusiastic buddy circle .. I even saved that ending picture for quite sometime now ..I can say with sufficient confidence that I have just reached the intermediate level from the beginner level now !There is still a long long interesting journey ahead !
Hope to use this space to capture some interesting games in the future !
