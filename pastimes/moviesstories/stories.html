Rubik's Cube

As a child I did see the Rubik's cube but thought it was never possible to solve this puzzle unless you rearrange the stickers . After growing up I realised there was another way to solve it,by opening up the cube and reassembling the pieces :) .. It was not until 2006 November after 2 years of cubing around I come to believe completely that the cube can be solved by a systematic algorithm ! Thanks to the internet I finally learnt to solve it with a decent speed to 7-9mins !I found Beginner's solution link particularly useful to get started
I found Mr Dan Brown's Videos on You tube very very useful ! There is this whole lot of techniques for speed cubing yet to be explored !
